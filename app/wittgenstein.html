Wittgenstein

Tractatus Logico-Philosophicus

This project was born with the intention to provide a key for a dynamic reading of Ludwig Wittgenstein's 'Tractatus logico-philosophicus', and to create a web page dedicated to educative purposes. The idea is to show the 7 principal propositions with the possibility to go deeper following their diagrammatical logic.

The 'Tractatus' itself implies the priority of certain statements over others, but the printed copy of the book prevents a dynamic use and shows every single proposition enunciated in its succession, thus losing the sense of priority during the reading. This website aims at providing a potential alternative.
